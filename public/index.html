About BottleOps

We started BottleOps with a simple idea: everyday essentials should be better — better for you, better for the planet, and better looking. Our stainless steel bottles and reusable straws are built to replace the disposable stuff that ends up in landfills.

Beyond products, we offer hands-on services like bottle refurbishment, straw cleaning, and professional decal application. We believe in keeping what you have in top shape rather than throwing it away.

Whether you're upgrading your daily carry or looking for a unique gift, BottleOps has you covered.
